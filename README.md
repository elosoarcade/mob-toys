# Mob Toys
Mob Toys MCBE Add-On adds small collectible toys of the game's mobs, as well as a new trader called Seller, who sells the Toy Box, in which you can obtain the figures, along with many other sought-after items at quite reasonable prices.

The Toy Box can only be opened with a golden pickaxe and will randomly drop one of the toys. Some, like the Zombie, are much more common, while the Creeper and the Witch are the rarest.

In this first edition, there are six figures available: Cow, Zombie, Sheep, Enderman, Witch and Creeper. More figures will be added in future updates.

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
